Bim services

A digital design and engineering company, we offer a wide array of BIM modeling services that cover residential, commercial, educational, industrial, and transportation projects. We also work with clients in healthcare, data centers, and retail.

Our goal is to develop new and optimize existing engineering solutions for architectural and construction projects. We tuned our digital design tools to deliver high-quality services on informational modeling of buildings (BIM) and virtual construction (VDC).

BIM modeling is what we excel at. The specifics of architecture and engineering requirements are at the core of every PowerKh project. We help bring down the labor intensity of your construction by automating and optimizing the production processes.

On average, we can make your routine tasks 5 times more efficient. With a focus on effective building designs, we strive to improve the way you calculate, build, and operate.

As a BIM company, we aim to deliver smart and practical solutions to facilitate the work of AEC (Architecture, Engineering & Construction) professionals.

Virtual design and construction by skilled professionals Why Powerkh?

VDC construction is a critical element of a successful building project. We offer virtual design and construction services to improve your team's planning, management, and performance. Now, your designers, architects, managers, and construction professionals can work together seamlessly.

At PowerKh, VDC experts cooperate to deliver accurate and aesthetic visual projects. We also use BIM software to integrate schedules and cost outlays on the design stage, increasing your business productivity. Thanks to our BIM services, your business performance will skyrocket.
